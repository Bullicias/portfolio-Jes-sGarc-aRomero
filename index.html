About me

I'm a 3D modeler and 3D/2D animator from Madrid, Spain, and I work on product advertisement, creating assets for video games, and creating special effects for film. I enjoy creating things that seem impossible and then seeing them come to fruition and be enjoyed by others.

My work involves creating animations for product advertisements, developing assets for video games, and creating realistic special effects for films. I also add a personal touch to your product and ensure it's appealing. My goal is to turn an idea into reality. I've created advertisements, assets, and special effects for many well-known brands.
